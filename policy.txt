CardioAnalyser Privacy Policy

Effective Date: November 16, 2025


Introduction

CardioAnalyser is an application designed to provide information about cardiovascular health. The app asks simple questions such as cholesterol level, blood pressure, age, and lifestyle habits to provide a cardiovascular risk assessment using a logistic regression model.


Data Collection

- CardioAnalyser does NOT collect, store, or share any personal information.
- All answers entered by users remain on the device and are only used to generate results within the app.
- No data is sent to servers or third-party services.


3. Data Usage

- The app only uses the input provided by the user to calculate a cardiovascular risk score.
- Results are shown directly to the user and are not recorded anywhere.
